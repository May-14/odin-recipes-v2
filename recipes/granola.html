description

the following recipe will show you how to make an incredibly delicious and healthy granola. each bite will taste of warm honey, cinnamon and vanilla, with the right mix of sweet and salty. made only of natural ingredients, done with either coconut or olive oil and sweetened exclusively with either maple syrup or honey, this granola is a perfect healthy snack or breakfast for everyone!

ingredients

  • 4 cups rolled oats
  • 2 cups of raw nuts
  • 1 cup of seeds
  • 3/4 tsp of salt
  • 1/2 tsp of cinnamon
  • 1 tsp of natural vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cups of coconut or olive oil
  • 1/2 cups of maple syrup or honey
  • optional ingredients:
    • 2/3 cups of dried fruits
    • 1/3 cups of another ingredient, such as chocolate or coconut

note: you can experiment with a bit less honey/maple syrup if you find it too sweet.

recipe

  1. preheat oven to 350°F or 177°C
  2. mix all dry ingredients including salt and cinnamon
  3. add the liquid ingredients to the dry ones and mix
  4. put the mix in a pan and put it in the oven
  5. after 12 1/2 minutes take the pan from the oven, give it a mix and put it in the oven once more
  6. after 12 1/2 minutes remove the pan from the oven
  7. let cool for half an hour
  8. enjoy!

note: storing in freezer keeps granola fresher for longer
